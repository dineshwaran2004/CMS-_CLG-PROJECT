About Us - Catering Management System

Welcome to the Catering Management System

The "Dashboard" is a catering management system designed to streamline operations, manage workers, and enhance overall efficiency. Our system helps simplify the process of worker registration and assignment.

Key Features

  • Easy worker registration with detailed forms.
  • Efficient task assignment for workers.
  • Streamlined communication via worker calling.
  • Centralized management for catering operations.

How It Works

  • Worker Registration: Workers fill out detailed forms with their personal information, skills, and availability.
  • Task Allocation: Based on the input data, managers can assign specific tasks to workers efficiently.
  • Worker Communication: The system enables direct calling to workers for real-time updates and instructions.

This innovative system ensures smooth operations and effective management for any catering business.
